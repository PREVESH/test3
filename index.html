Workshop on Internet of Things (IoT)

We are pleased to inform you that the School of Engineering and Technology (SET), Mody University, is organizing a Workshop on the Internet of Things (IoT) on 25th May 2025 at our campus.

We cordially invite your esteemed institution to participate in this event. The workshop is designed to provide students with practical knowledge and hands-on experience in IoT technologies, which are highly relevant in today’s evolving technical landscape.

Fee Details:

  • A nominal registration fee of Rs. 500/- will be applicable.
  • FREE participation for all final-year (III Year) Polytechnic students.
  • Free participants must upload a scanned copy of their valid student ID card through the registration form.

We kindly request you to circulate this information among your students and encourage them to register at the earliest.
